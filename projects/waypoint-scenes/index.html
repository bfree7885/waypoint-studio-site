Waypoint Scenes

A tool for building layered, parallax environmental scenes from real-world landscapes.

What It Is

Waypoint Scenes is a tool for building layered, parallax environmental scenes from real-world landscapes.

Purpose

  • Encourage attention to place
  • Help users reconnect with landscape
  • Provide a quiet, creative outlet grounded in real environments

Features

  • Upload landscape photos
  • Separate into foreground, midground, background
  • Create subtle depth and motion
Focused on long-term development of geospatial and resilience systems.
